# 08 — Mock Interview

**TL;DR:** A staged mock for a technical/system-design round at KoiReader. Two ways
to use it:

1. **Solo:** open [questions.md](questions.md), answer each out loud / on paper
   *before* looking, then check [model-answers.md](model-answers.md) and grade
   yourself with [rubric.md](rubric.md).
2. **Live with me (recommended):** ask me in chat — *"run the KoiReader mock
   interview"* — and I'll play interviewer: one question at a time, follow-ups based
   on your answers, then a scored debrief. That's closer to the real thing because
   I'll push on weak spots.

## Format (mirrors a real 45–60 min technical round)

| Stage | Time | What it tests |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Warm-up / background | 5 min | Your narrative, fit for systems-first CV |
| 2. Rapid-fire fundamentals | 10 min | Streaming, codecs, decode, GIL — recall under pressure |
| 3. Deep dive | 10 min | One topic to real depth (usually inference or fault tolerance) |
| 4. System design | 20 min | The multi-camera pipeline + curveballs |
| 5. Your questions | 5 min | Signals seniority and genuine interest |
