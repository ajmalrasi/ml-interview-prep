## How design rounds are graded

They're not looking for the "right" diagram. They score: do you **clarify
requirements**, **state assumptions**, **reason about tradeoffs**, **find
bottlenecks**, and **handle failure**? Your resume's "ambiguous problems with no
obvious answer" is exactly this muscle — show the *method*, think out loud.
